How AIviary works

Our Mission

AIviary exists to help people discover the right AI tools for their needs.

The AI landscape is growing rapidly, making it increasingly difficult to identify which tools are genuinely useful and which are simply generating hype. Our goal is to simplify that process by providing a structured, accessible directory that helps users explore and compare AI tools with confidence.

How Tools Are Selected

Tools featured on AIviary are chosen based on factors such as:

  • Relevance to a specific use case or category
  • Popularity within their market
  • Innovation and unique functionality
  • User interest and industry adoption
  • Overall usefulness for individuals or businesses

We aim to include a wide range of tools across categories such as:

  • Writing & Documents
  • Design & Media
  • Learning
  • Data & Analytics
  • Assistants
  • Audio & Voice
  • Development & Code
  • Research
  • Finance & Operations
  • Office Tools
  • Career & Job Tools

The inclusion of a tool does not constitute an endorsement.

How Recommendations Work

AIviary recommendations are designed to help users discover tools that may fit their goals, workflows and preferences.

Recommendations are generated using information available within the AIviary platform, including:

  • Tool categories
  • Features and capabilities
  • Use cases
  • User-selected preferences

Recommendations are intended as guidance only and should not be considered professional advice or guarantees of suitability.

Are Rankings Sponsored?

No.

AIviary does not currently accept payment in exchange for higher rankings, preferential placement or recommendation priority.

Tools are organised based on directory structure, categories, filtering systems and recommendation logic rather than paid promotion.

If sponsored listings or advertising are introduced in the future, they will be clearly identified.

Do Companies Pay to Be Listed?

Currently, listings are not purchased and companies do not pay for inclusion.

In the future, AIviary may introduce optional commercial opportunities such as:

  • Sponsored placements
  • Featured listings
  • Advertising partnerships

Any paid placement will be clearly disclosed so that users can distinguish promotional content from editorial content.

Tool Information

We make reasonable efforts to keep tool information accurate and up to date.

However, AI tools change frequently. Features, pricing, availability and functionality may change without notice.

Users should always verify information directly with the tool provider before making purchasing or business decisions.

Independence and Transparency

AIviary aims to remain a transparent and user-focused discovery platform.

Our objective is to help users find the right tool for their needs rather than promote specific products.

Where commercial relationships exist, they will be disclosed clearly and openly.

Feedback

The AI ecosystem evolves quickly, and we welcome feedback from users.

If you spot outdated information, missing tools or inaccuracies, please get in touch so we can continue improving the platform for everyone.
