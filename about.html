About AIviary

AIviary is your one-stop platform for discovering the world’s smartest AI tools. Whether you’re a creator, student, or business professional, AIviary makes it simple to explore and find AI models tailored to your needs. We aggregate models from multiple sources into a single, easy-to-browse library.

How It Works

Browse categories, search for a task, or explore random models to find the perfect AI tool. Each model links directly to its source—whether it’s a dedicated website or a platform like Hugging Face—so you can start using it immediately. AIviary does not host or run any AI models; we provide a curated directory to simplify your discovery process.

Future Updates & Version History

AIviary is constantly evolving. Here’s a glimpse of what’s coming and what we’ve already launched:

  • v1.0 – Initial public launch: core categories, search, random model discovery.
  • v1.1 – Improved search relevance and category filtering, minor UI tweaks.
  • v1.2 – Added new AI categories and expanded model dataset.
  • v2.0 (Planned) – Personalized recommendations, user accounts, and bookmarking models.
  • v2.1 (Planned) – Monetization features: affiliate links, ads, and optional premium content.

Future updates are designed to be simple to use while improving the discovery experience. Stay tuned!
